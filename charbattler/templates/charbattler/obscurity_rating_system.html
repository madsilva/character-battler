The Obscurity Rating System

The Obscurity Rating System (ORS) is our patented* method of keeping Character Battler fun and interesting. Every character is assigned a rating from 0-3, representing how well known they are.

0: Almost universally known characters. These are ones you could ask any random person on the street about and expect them to know. A universe probably only has one or two of these, if any. Examples: Spongebob, Superman, Mario.

1: Characters that would be known by people with a passing familiarity to the series. People who have heard of the series or perhaps read/watched/played only part of it.

2: Characters that would be known by people who have read/watched/played all of the media. Think secondary characters.

3: Characters that would only be known by people who have intimate/detailed knowledge of the media.
